Harry Potter Number Matching Potion Game

Instructions:
You want to help the characters add the right volumes to make a potion.
The potion total volume goal is displayed under these instructions
You will be given a random number at the start of the game for the total volume.
There are four characters below. By clicking on a character you will add a specific amount of volume to your total volume.
You win the game by matching your total volume to the random number, and lose the game if your total volume goes above the random number.
The value that each character has is hidden from you until you click on it.
Each time when the game starts, the game will change the values of each character. Good luck and make some potions for the Gryffindors!
